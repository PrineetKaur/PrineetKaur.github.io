About Me

I come with a decades' experience of Innovating and Building at the crossroads of Product, Engineering, and Data (delivering ambitious roadmaps, and untangling unstructured territoires within fast-paced scale-ups).

WHAT DRIVES ME?

“Turning technical chaos into clear, scalable products that users actually use”, and “Helping teams meet deadlines and ship stuff they’re genuinely proud of.”

My career so far has been a blend of managing Technology Products and leading Complex Technical Programs. And lately, my work has revolved around building Developer-Focused Products (developer experience, infrastructure, platform capabilities) and data platforms (data science, engineering, analytics).

Along the way, I’ve collaborated closely with DevOps, System Architects, and Platform/Data Engineering teams. And these are the kind of workspaces that keep me the Happiest, closely contributing to "How Engineers build, and use Technical Products". Further, I love to tinker with Hands-on Experiments (on Github), and thereby contribute to the Open Source Community.

Somewhere between all of that, I also developed a soft spot for Technical Storytelling. Sharing my thoughts (on Medium) through Blogs on the Latest Tech Advancements and Technical Writeups (e.g. how-to guides for developer onboarding or breaking down complex concepts in a way that lands).

MY PHILOSOPHY:

Build smart, deliver well, stay curious, and make technology understandable for all!

Outside of work, I love to keep myself Active and Fit. (Since school days, I have been an active athlete, won many medals, participated in marathons, and represented my state twice at national-level tournament).
